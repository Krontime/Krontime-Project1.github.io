What is this E.D.M.?

'EDM' (AKA: Electric Dance Music or Club Music) is a broad range of percussive electronic music genres made largely for nightclubs, raves, and festivals. EDM is generally produced for playback by disc jockeys (DJs) who create seamless selections of tracks, called a set, by segueing from one recording to another. EDM producers mostly perform their music live in a concert or festival.
